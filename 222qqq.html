Mineplex Missions & 1.12 Support

Sigils LTGDo posted Fri at 15:06

Hey Gang! Got a neat little update for you this weekend. We've added Mineplex Missions to the main Lobby. Mineplex Missions are Daily Challenges that you can complete to earn bonus Treasure Shards, Experience and more! Every day 5 new Missions will be offered for you to acquire. However, you can only have 5 Missions active on your account at one time, so you'll need to complete Missions to be able to get the new ones. There are several hundred Missions that are from selected randomly so try to collect them all.  Look for the Slime right by the spawn to get started on your next Mission!

Additionally 1.12 was supposed to come out today. (It is now delayed according to Mojang) but we now have support implemented so that you will be able to connect with 1.12 clients when the update is released. That's right we now support clients from 1.8 <-> 1.12! Hope you guys have a great weekend, and enjoy Missions. Leave your feedback over on the forums.

Castle Assault Release!

SamitoD YT posted May 12, 17


Hello everyone! Today we released a new game mode called Castle Assault! This is a new style of PvP game that Mineplex hasn’t seen for awhile! This game is an objective based Vanilla PvP game that contains potions, fishing rods, golden apples, and more fan favorite vanilla items that players love to PvP with! In order to win the game you must destroy both of the enemy sentry crystals & slay the enemy king in a fast paced, combat friendly 8v8 game! The first team to kill the enemy king wins! This game introduces a few new twists to the current Mineplex system that many of you love, a few of the new twists are:

 

  • Tiered Achievement System (very rewarding in higher tiers!)
  • Crowns Currency (Instead of gems FOR THIS GAME ONLY!)
  • New Style of Kit Leveling/Progression (No Kit Level requirement to purchase upgrades)
  • Vanilla Knockback Engine (Instead of the standard Mineplex Engine)
  • Kill Streak Rewards
  • Leaderboards (Overall Kills/Wins & Daily Kills/Wins)


Be sure to hop on and try out this fast paced action packed PvP game ONLY on Mineplex! We will be actively updating this game and listening to your feedback, so be sure to give it a try and tell us what you think! If you want to read more details about the game, click HERE!
